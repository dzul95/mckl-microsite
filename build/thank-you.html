About MCKL

Welcome to Methodist College Kuala Lumpur
An Institution where values and tradition meet excellence in education!

For over three decades, Methodist College Kuala Lumpur (MCKL) has been providing quality, holistic education in the best of Methodist tradition. MCKL fully embraces the challenges and opportunities of educating and preparing students for further education, professional development, and job careers in the new IR 4.0 environment.
