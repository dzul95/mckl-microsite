Course Description

  • This course will give you an overview and insight of requirements of popular universities in the world, and what would be some of the requirements to possibly gain admission to them.

  • It will also take you through pointers for writing admission essays/personal statements, resume-writing, scholarship applications, and interview tips.

  • At the end of the course, you would be more prepared to apply to the university of your choice, and stand a higher chance to get in.
What will students learn in the course?
  • Entry requirements for top universities in the world (UK, USA, Singapore, Australia, Hong Kong, and some others)
  • Writing the admission essays / personal statement
  • Creating a useful and relevant CV
  • Applying for scholarships to universities and external companies
  • Interview tips for universities and scholarships
Are there any course requirements or prerequisites?
  • Passionate to explore university options around the world
Who can join the course?
  • Year 10-11 (Form 4-5) and Pre-University Students

Name

Mr Joshua Johnson

Biography

Joshua has been working with tertiary students at different capacities since graduating from university. His current role for the past 9 years is to counsel, help, engage and interact with pre-university students, as they decide on what to study at university and which university to go to.

He has (successfully) placed students in top universities in the world including the ones in UK, USA, Australia, New Zealand, Hong Kong and Singapore. ‘Finding the right fit’ is his motto, to ensure all students have a chance for tertiary education despite their background and circumstances.

Joshua received his Bachelor of Communication (Hons.) degree from Universiti Sains Malaysia, Penang. Having passion to engage with young people, he worked in an NGO for 7.5 years that trained tertiary students, and for the past 9.5 years has been in the University Placement Department of Methodist College Kuala Lumpur. Currently, he heads the department, and frequently engages with students, alumni, universities and other stakeholders.
